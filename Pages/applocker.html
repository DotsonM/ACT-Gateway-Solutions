ACT GATEWAY: AppLocker Considerations

If your Testing Center is utilizing AppLocker policy to secure Windows devices, you may notice issues with ACT Gateway. Specifically, default AppLocker policies have been found to suppress a necessary script that runs during Device Checks on the machine during pretest validation and during testing sessions.

If you are applying Default AppLocker policies and you find that your Device Check is falsely returning a failed “Device Check” due to an outdated OS version, consider adding the AppLocker rule below to allow the check to properly process on the machine.

AppLocker Rule to Add for Scripts

  1. Right-click the “Script Rules” and choose “New Rule”.
  2. On the “Before you Begin” window, click Next.
  3. On the “Permissions” window, ensure the Action is set to Allow and the User or Group is Everyone, click Next.
    Path and click Next.
  4. On the “Path” window, enter %OSDRIVE%\Users\*\appdata\local\Temp\* and click Next.
  5. On the “Exceptions” window, click Next.
  6. On the “Name” window, enter a name for the rule and click Create.
  7. Test the rule to ensure that ACT Gateway Device Check is now working.
  8. When ready, right-click on AppLocker, Export Policy and deploy to other Testing Center machines (likely done through Group Policy).
